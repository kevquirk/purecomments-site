# Coming soon!

Managed hosting for Pure Comments is on the way. It’ll give you all the benefits of the self-hosted version without the need to run your own server or worry about updates, security, or maintenance. You’ll get a fast, reliable, privacy-respecting comment service that just works, while still keeping full ownership of your data.

The goal is to offer a simple, predictable service for personal sites, blogs, and small web projects. No tracking, no ads, no upsell funnels. Just a lightweight hosted version of Pure Comments that stays true to the principles of the [Pure Commons](https://purecommons.org) project, for a minimal monthly fee.

More details will be available soon.
