Pure Comments lets you add clean, lightweight comments to **any website**. There no tracking, no ads, and no bloat. Pure Comments is built for personal publishers who want a straightforward way for readers to join the conversation.

Just [add the embed code](/getting-started-with-pure-comments#embed) where you want comments to appear on your site, and you're good to go.

## Why Pure Comments?

Pure Comments follows the same principles as the rest of [Pure Commons](https://purecommons.org):

- Simple to add to **any website**
- Privacy-first
- No analytics, profiling, or third-party scripts
- Works with any site that can embed a small snippet
- Fully self-hosted and open source

If you want comments without the usual noise, this is for you.

## Features

- An easy to use admin dashboard for managing comments.
- Ability to reply to comments as author from admin dashboard.
- Anti-spam mechanism within the comment form.
- Customisable design so comments can look like the rest of your site's design.
- Configurable email notifications via Amazon SES.
